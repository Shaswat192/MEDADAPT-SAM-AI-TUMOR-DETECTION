MEDADAPT-SAM: AUTOMATIC PROMPT-DRIVEN BRAIN TUMOR SEGMENTATION

Shaswat Pradhan, Nikhil Kumar

OBJECTIVES

  • Automated Precision: Develop a prompt-driven system using Segment Anything Model (SAM) for BraTS 2021 dataset.
  • Domain Adaptation: Implementation of specialized bottleneck adapters for high-fidelity medical imaging features.
  • Uncertainty Guidance: Integration of Monte Carlo Dropout to identify and refine ambiguous segmentation boundaries.
  • Clinical Intelligence: Automated clinical report generation and visualization for immediate diagnostic support.
